26. paano nakakaapekto ang heograpiya sa kasaysayan


(I'm sorry, I'm not too great at Filipino so I'll answer in English)

Geography can affect history in many different ways. It affects the countries/ groups of people that are able to interact with one another and determines the relationship between them. It can also be linked to the kind of environment a certain place has which affects the way civilizations formed and people thrived in these places. Some concrete examples of Philippine geography affecting history includes our land being fertile and good for planting which resulted in us being a constant target for natural resources (i.e. The Spanish, American and Japanese periods) and the eventual attempts of cultivation such as the construction of the banaue rice terraces. Another very relevant example of geography affecting history is how Mindanao was not really involved in the colonization by Spain due to the many mountain ranges that protected it from the venturing spaniards.
