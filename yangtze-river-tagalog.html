1. Kilala bilang Yangtze River


Answer:

Ang Yangtze o Yangzi ay ang pinakamahabang ilog sa Asya, ang pangatlong pinakamahabang sa mundo at ang pinakamahaba sa mundo na ganap na dumaloy sa loob ng isang bansa. Tumataas ito sa Jari Hill sa Tanggula Mountains (Tibetan Plateau) at dumadaloy 6,300 km (3,900 mi) sa isang pangkalahatang direksyon sa East China Sea. Ito ang pang-anim na pinakamalaking ilog sa pamamagitan ng dami ng paglabas sa buong mundo. Ang drainage basin nito ay binubuo ng ikalimang bahagi ng lupain ng Tsina, at tahanan ng halos isang-katlo ng populasyon ng bansa


2. Ano ang Yangtze river​


Answer:

The Yangtze or Yangzi is the longest river in Asia, the third-longest in the world and the longest in the world to flow entirely within one country. It rises at Jari Hill in the Tanggula Mountains and flows 6,300 km in a generally easterly direction to the East China Sea.

4. pinakamahabang ilog sa asya at kilala rin bilang yangtze river​


Answer:

The Yangtze or Yangzi (English: /ˈjæŋtsi/ or /ˈjɑːŋtsi/) is the longest river in Asia, the third-longest in the world and the longest in the world to flow entirely within one country. It rises at Jari Hill in the Tanggula Mountains (Tibetan Plateau) and flows 6,300 km (3,900 mi) in a generally easterly direction to the East China Sea. It is the sixth-largest river by discharge volume in the world. Its drainage basin comprises one-fifth of the land area of China, and is home to nearly one-third of the country's population.[7]

16. Anong ibang tawag sa Yangtze River​


Answer:

Chang jiang

Explanation:

Yangtze, which means "child of the ocean," is a name primarily used by Westerners. In China, the river is referred to as Chang Jiang, meaning "long river," while the name Yangtze is reserved for the small section of the river near its mouth.
