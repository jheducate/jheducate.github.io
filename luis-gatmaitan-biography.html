14. what is a BIOGRAPHY? BIOGRAPHY- an account of someone's lifewritten by someone else. write your biography on the space provided.


Answer:

Explanation:

A biography, or simply bio, is a detailed description of a person's life. It involves more than just the basic facts like education, work, relationships, and death; it portrays a person's experience of these life events. Unlike a profile or curriculum vitae (résumé), a biography presents a subject's life story, highlighting various aspects of their life, including intimate details of experience, and may include an analysis of the subject's personality.

Biographical works are usually non-fiction, but fiction can also be used to portray a person's life. One in-depth form of biographical coverage is called legacy writing. Works in diverse media, from literature to film, form the genre known as biography.

An authorized biography is written with the permission, cooperation, and at times, participation of a subject or a subject's heirs. An autobiography is written by the person himself or herself, sometimes with the assistance of a collaborator or ghostwriter.

Ano ang pilosopiyang pilipino ng sandaang dosenang sapatos ni Luis Gatmaitan?

20. read and write a summary about the short a dozen pairs of shoes by Luis P Gatmaitan​


Answer:

"A Dozen Pairs of Shoes" by Luis P. Gatmaitan is a heartwarming story about a young boy named Jesse who lives in the countryside with his family. Jesse is fascinated by the different shoes worn by the people he meets and longs to have a pair of his own. However, his family struggles to make ends meet, and buying shoes is a luxury they cannot afford. Despite this, Jesse remains hopeful and resourceful, finding ways to make his own makeshift shoes out of materials like banana leaves and rubber tires. Through his struggles, Jesse learns the value of hard work and determination, and ultimately discovers that happiness can come from the simple pleasures in life. The story is a touching reminder of the importance of resilience and creativity in the face of adversity.
