Risk factors for TB include:

•Poverty.

•HIV infection.

•Homelessness.

•Being in jail or prison (where close contact can spread infection)

•Substance abuse.

•Taking medication that weakens the immune system.

•Kidney disease and diabetes.

•Organ transplants.

Tuberculosis is caused by bacteria that spread from person to person through microscopic droplets released into the air. This can happen when someone with the untreated, active form of tuberculosis coughs, speaks, sneezes, spits, laughs or sings. Although tuberculosis is contagious, it's not easy to catch.

26. paano naipapasa ang sakit na pathogens​


Maraming paraan kung paano nakakahawa ang pathogens. Ito ay depende sa kung anong klase ito. Maaaring mapasa ang sakit sa pamamagitan ng skin contact, bodily fluids, airborne particles, contact with feces, and touching a surface touched by an infected person.

source: www.healthline.com

Answer:

TAGALOG:

Ang mga pathogens ay maaaring mailipat ng ilang mga paraan depende sa uri. Maaari silang kumalat sa pamamagitan ng pakikipag-ugnay sa balat, mga likido sa katawan, mga particle na nasa hangin, makipag-ugnay sa mga dumi, at hawakan ang isang ibabaw na hinawakan ng isang taong nahawahan.

ENGLISH:

Pathogens can be transmitted a few ways depending on the type. They can be spread through skin contact, bodily fluids, airborne particles, contact with feces, and touching a surface touched by an infected person.
