Answer:

1. Saan inihalintulad ni Rizal ang salitang kalayaan?

- Ibong nasa himpapawid

- Sa bayan o kanyang mga kababayan(satin)

- Hayop at malansang isda

- Dahil ibig niyang sabihin na ang ating sariling wika ay ang naghahatol/maghahatol kung ano ang pagkakakilanlan natin o sa kung sino at ano tayo, sa bayan man, nayon o kaharian.

- Latin, Ingles, Kastila at salitang angel

Answer:

Trees

BY JOYCE KILMER

I think that I shall never see

A poem lovely as a tree.

A tree whose hungry mouth is prest

Against the earth’s sweet flowing breast;

A tree that looks at God all day,

And lifts her leafy arms to pray;

A tree that may in Summer wear

A nest of robins in her hair;

Upon whose bosom snow has lain;

Who intimately lives with rain.

Poems are made by fools like me,

But only God can make a tree.
