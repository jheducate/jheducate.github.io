Answer:

The love of a mother is not just a written word on paper or a phrase that is easily said. It is a true feeling of humility, sacrifice, and care that never fades no matter what happens.

From the moment of pregnancy to soothing her children at night, a mother is willing to give everything for their welfare. She needs strength and endurance to meet the needs of her children, especially if she is alone. She does not stop worrying about them and their health, and she does not lag behind in teaching them lessons that will make them virtuous in the future.

The love of a mother does not end with her children either. She is willing to help her husband, her family, and her community in any way possible. She does not think of herself and does not hesitate to give support to those in need.

A mother is not just a person who gives life to her children. She is a judge, a friend, a teacher, and a servant at all times. She needs patience, skill, and trust to be a good mother, and despite all the challenges that come her way, she continues to give love at all times.

The love of a mother is not easy to explain in words. It is a feeling that flows all the time, and it is a gift that cannot be paid for any amount. Despite all the trials and challenges in life, a mother continues to uphold her family and give unending love to them.
