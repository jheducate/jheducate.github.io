13. Ano ang pagkakaiba ng natural na liwanag sa artipisyal na liwanag?​


Answer:

Natural light doesn't come from electricity and it is not man-made (except for the fire), its examples are sun, fire, stars, and animals that makes light with their bodies. Artificial light on the other hand, is man-made, it is made by electricity, for example: light bulb, cellphones, flashlight, etc. So that means natural light cannot be made by humans, (except fire, but fire is still a natural source of light) and artificial light is man-made.
