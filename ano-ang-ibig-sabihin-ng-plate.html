  • 29. Ano Ang Ibig Sabihin Ng Plate Tectonics (Hindi Teorya)

1. Ano ang ibig sabihin ng Plate Tectonic?


Answer:

Plate tectonics is the theory that Earth's outer shell is divided into several plates that glide over the mantle, the rocky inner layer above the core.

Explanation:

The plates act like a hard and rigid shell compared to Earth's mantle. This strong outer layer is called the lithosphere, which is 100 km (60 miles) thick, according to Encyclopedia Britannica. 

Answer:

It means tectonic plates.Ang plate tectonics ay isang teoryang pang-agham na naglalarawan sa malakihang galaw ng pitong malalaking plate at ang paggalaw ng mas malaking bilang ng mas maliit na mga plate ng lithosphere ng Earth, dahil nagsimula ang mga proseso ng tektoniko sa Earth sa pagitan ng 3.3 at 3.5 bilyong taon na ang nakalilipas.


2. ano ang ibig sabihin ng slide plate​


A slide plate is linear bearing such as may be part of the expansion joints of bridges, high temperature horizontal ducts of water-tube boilers and other mechanical or structural engineering applications. In each case one plate is fixed and the other slides on top as expansion or contraction occurs.

29. Ano Ang Ibig Sabihin Ng Plate Tectonics (Hindi Teorya)


Plate tectonics is a theory formulated to explain the phenomena of continental drift and sea floor spreading, and the formation of major physical features of the Earth's surface.
