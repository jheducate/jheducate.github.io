30. Ano ang kahulogan ng mga acronym


Answer:

An acronym is a word formed by abbreviating a phrase by combining certain letters of words in the phrase (often the first initial of each) into a single term. Common examples of acronyms include NASA (an acronym for National Aeronautics and Space Administration) and FOMO (a slang acronym for fear of missing out).  Usually connectives or words such as 'and' or 'of' are not included in the abbreviation. Examples: NASA = National Aeronautics (and) Space Administration. DIY = Do It Yourself.
