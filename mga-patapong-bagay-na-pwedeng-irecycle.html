28. ano ang mga pwedeng irecycle?


Answer:

1. Paper: Paper products such as newspapers, magazines, office paper, and cardboard can be recycled. Paper recycling helps to conserve trees, reduce landfill space, and save energy.

2. Plastic: Many types of plastic can be recycled, including bottles, containers, bags, and packaging materials. Recycling plastic helps to reduce the amount of plastic waste that ends up in landfills and oceans.

3. Glass: Glass bottles and jars can be recycled to create new glass products. Recycling glass helps to conserve natural resources and reduce greenhouse gas emissions.

4. Metal: Metals such as aluminum, steel, and copper can be recycled. Recycling metal helps to conserve natural resources and reduce energy consumption.

5. Electronics: Electronic devices such as computers, cell phones, and televisions can be recycled. Recycling electronics helps to recover valuable metals and reduce electronic waste.

6. Batteries: Batteries can be recycled to recover valuable metals and prevent them from ending up in landfills.

7. Textiles: Clothing and other textiles can be recycled or repurposed to create new products.

8. Organic Waste: Organic waste such as food scraps and yard waste can be composted to create nutrient-rich soil for gardening.
