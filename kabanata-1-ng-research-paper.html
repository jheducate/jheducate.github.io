11. hi sino marunong gumawa ng research title, research paper?​


Here are some tips on creating a research title and research paper:

Choose a topic that interests you: It is easier to write about a topic that you are interested in. Choose a topic that you are passionate about or curious to learn more about.

Narrow down the topic: Once you have a general idea of what you want to write about, narrow down the topic. This will help you to focus your research and make your paper more specific.

Conduct thorough research: Conduct thorough research on your topic. Use reliable sources such as academic journals, books, and government websites.

Develop a thesis statement: Your thesis statement should be a clear and concise statement that summarizes the main point of your research paper.

Create an outline: An outline will help you to organize your thoughts and structure your paper.

Write a rough draft: Write a rough draft of your research paper. Don't worry about grammar or spelling at this stage.

Edit and revise: Once you have a rough draft, edit and revise your paper. Check for grammar and spelling errors and make sure that your paper is well-organized and easy to read.

Write a clear and concise title: Your research title should be clear and concise. It should give the reader an idea of what your paper is about.

Use a consistent citation style: Use a consistent citation style throughout your paper. This will make it easier for readers to follow your sources.

Proofread your paper: Before submitting your paper, proofread it carefully. Check for grammar and spelling errors and make sure that your paper is well-organized and easy to read.
