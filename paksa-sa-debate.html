17. Magtala ng 5 napapanahong issue na maaring paksa sa isang debate​


Answer:

1. Climate change and its impact on the environment and human society.

2. The ongoing COVID-19 pandemic and its effects on public health and the economy.

3. Income inequality and the ways in which it affects social and economic mobility.

4. Immigration policies and the rights and treatment of immigrants and refugees.

5. The role of technology in society and its impact on privacy, security and democracy

Explanation:

1. Climate change is a pressing issue that affects the entire planet. It includes rising temperatures, sea level rise, and more intense storms, droughts, and floods. These changes have significant impacts on food and water security, human health, and the environment. The debate on this issue centers around how to mitigate the effects of climate change through policies such as carbon pricing, renewable energy, and conservation.

2. The COVID-19 pandemic is a global health crisis that has affected nearly every aspect of society. It has led to widespread illness and death, as well as economic downturns and job losses. Debates around this issue focus on the best ways to slow the spread of the virus, including through lockdowns, social distancing, and vaccine distribution.

3. Income inequality is a persistent problem in many societies, with a small number of people controlling a large share of wealth and resources. This can lead to a lack of access to basic needs, such as housing, education, and healthcare, for those in the lower income brackets. Debate around this issue often centers on how to reduce income inequality through policies such as progressive taxation, minimum wage increases, and social safety net programs.

4. Immigration policies are a contentious issue in many countries, with debates often centered on how to balance the rights and needs of immigrants and refugees with those of citizens. Issues include border security, pathways to citizenship, and the treatment of those seeking asylum.

5. The role of technology in society is a rapidly evolving issue, with many concerns around its impact on privacy, security, and democracy. Debates around this issue often center on the best ways to regulate and control the use of technology, including through data protection laws and internet censorship.
