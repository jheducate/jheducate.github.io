5. Were is purao island is​


Answer:

Purao Island is located in Palawan, Philippines.

Answer:

The province of Palawan is rapidly emerging as one of the premier ecotourism destinations in the Philippines.

Undoubtedly, its tourism potential is beefed-up by the presence of the world famous Saint Paul Subterranean River (Underground River) in Puerto Princesa City - one of the New Seven Wonders of Nature, the Tubbataha Reef off the coast of Cagayancillo and the world-class resorts in El Nido and Coron.

Most of these places are located in the north. To get to these places, you have to pass through Roxas-the gateway municipality towards northern Palawan. It is right here where Purao Island is located.

Further, the municipality of Roxas is an hour drive to the municipality of San Vicente- dubbed as the next Boracay in Palawan-where an airport is due for completion to accommodate the influx of tourists that are expected to visit this township. With these developments Purao Island in Roxas is ideal for a high-end resort development and could be packaged as one of the premier island-resort destinations in northern Palawan.

Explanation:

The middle portion of the island is planted with coconuts. Natural vegetation in the island consists of native palms and a thin patch of mangrove trees. A shallow-dug well provided fresh water in the island
